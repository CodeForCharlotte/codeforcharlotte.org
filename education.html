Educational Resources

Where to Go to Learn How to Do What We Do

IT-oLogy

IT-oLogy is a non-profit, national organization committed to growing the IT talent pipeline and advancing the IT profession.

code.org

Code.org® is a non-profit dedicated to expanding participation in computer science by making it available in more schools, and increasing participation by women and underrepresented students of color.

Tech Talent South

Offers beginner focused 8-week programs to empower students with the skills to code, design, and ship dynamic web applications.

Kids-N-Technology

We are the original camp for ”build your own computer and take it home!" Building on our success, we offer multiple programs to meet the schedules and interests of students, parents, and teachers.

Treehouse

Learn from over 1000 videos created by our expert teachers on web design, coding, business, and much more. Charlotte Mecklenberg Library card holders can use the basic service for free

Girl Develop It! Charlotte

Logical Advantage Tech Talks

We meet monthly during lunch to discuss and share information on a variety of technology topics centered around the web, mobile, cloud, or software architecture. We offer an excellent way to receive a technical education and meet with peers to get more out of the latest platforms, products, technologies, and resources.

Skookum Free Friday Tech Talks

Laid-back, bi-weekly discussions on technology or innovation topics ranging from robotics to the future of meat.

Hackerspace Charlotte

Charlotte UX

Charlotte UX is for anybody interested in learning more about creating a great user experience. Join us once a month as we get together to discuss user experience design, information architecture, copywriting, analytics, usability studies, sketching and wireframing and much more.

Charlotte Front-End

Dedicated to best practices, semantic markup, progressive enhancement & graceful degradation. Over the course of time and quite often we discuss HTML5, CSS, cross-browser support & testing, mobile, optimization techniques and much more.

Charlotte ALT.NET

A group of Charlotte developers interested in .NET and sharing knowledge about the best in software technology and practices.

Charlotte Javascript

The home for JavaScript enthusiasts in Charlotte, North Carolina. We are a group of developers and designers that get together and chit chat about the most used and misunderstood scripting language around.

Charlotte Python

Meet other Python users, developers, and enthusiasts of all skill levels interested in discovering and sharing why the Python programming language is such a joy to work with.

Charlotte Ruby

Meet other Ruby programmers in Charlotte, and across the region.

Queen City PHP

The official Charlotte PHP users group. We're a group of PHP developers and enthusiasts in Charlotte, NC.
